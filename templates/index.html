TEXT SUMMARIZATION

Welcome to Automatic Summarization of Zambian Legislative Documents

Automatic summarization is the process of reducing a document or a set of documents to a shortened version while retaining the most important information. It can help users quickly understand the key points and save time in reading lengthy documents.

In this application, you can input or paste a link to a PDF containing legislative documents from Zambia, and the system will automatically generate a summary for you.

For more information on Zambian legislative documents, you can download PDF files from the official website: Zambian Acts of Parliament.
